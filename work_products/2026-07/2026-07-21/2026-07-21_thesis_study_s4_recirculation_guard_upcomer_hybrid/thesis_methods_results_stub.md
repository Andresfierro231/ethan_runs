# Thesis S4 Recirculation Guard Methods And Results Stub

## Methods

The S4 study joins the Phase 4 upcomer exchange/internal-Nu gate, the
no-solver exchange-evidence preflight, the LitRev throughflow-plus-recirculation
interface, and the gated ordinary single-stream branch precheck. The join is
classification-only: no coefficient, threshold, source family, or model form is
fit or selected.

## Results

The current evidence disables ordinary one-stream `Nu`, `f_D`, and `K` use for
the reviewed upcomer/branch rows. The package reviews
`90` ordinary single-stream
candidates and admits `0` ordinary rows. It also preserves
`45` reverse-flow/exchange diagnostic rows
as recirculation evidence, not calibration evidence. The future scoreable lane
is a throughflow-plus-recirculation exchange cell after `V_recirc`,
`mdot_exchange`, `tau_recirc`, same-window pressure/energy residuals, and
same-QOI uncertainty are admitted.

## Claim Boundary

This is a guard study, not a final scorecard. Recirculating rows can support a
thesis claim that ordinary one-stream closure labels fail today, but they cannot
support an ordinary upcomer `Nu`, `f_D`, `K`, F6, or exchange-cell coefficient.
