## Current State

Implementation complete and verified. F3_shah_apparent is live in friction_closures.py.

Key finding: At TAMU loop conditions (x+ = 0.04-0.6), F3_shah_apparent gives
28-99% more total pressure drop than F3_hagenbach. The Hagenbach correction (K_∞=1.33)
is the ASYMPTOTIC value for x+→∞ and underestimates total ΔP in the developing
regime because it does not capture the enhanced distributed friction during
profile development. Shah (1978) correctly combines both effects.

Muzychka & Yovanovich (2009) NOT implemented: exact constants for the extended
form were not verified from the primary source (scientific rigor requirement).
Documented as a caveat in the docstring.
