## Interpretation

Use `osc_mean` as the estimate, `unc_sem_corrected` as the corrected SEM of the
mean, `osc_rmse_about_trend` as the detrended oscillation component, and
`fit_slope` / `drift_over_window` as residual drift evidence. The derived
`paper_envelope_abs` is only a conservative display bound:
`1.96*SEM + detrended RMS + half-window drift`.

Salt2 and Salt3 mainline `total_Q` are screened out as not steady because the
net residual is near zero and drifting in relative terms. Salt4 mainline
`total_Q` passes the steady screen. For `total_Q`, use absolute W components
instead of relative percentages.
