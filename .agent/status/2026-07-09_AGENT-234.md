## Redesign (user feedback: figures looked crowded/ugly)

First cut had text-on-text collisions and a broken diverging chart. Root causes and fixes:
- **Case label collided with first span label** (same column, 6px apart) → replaced with
  section-header rows spanning the plot.
- **Diverging decomposition chart drew overlapping marks**: positive buoyancy (heater, +5 Pa)
  was painted on top of the friction bar at the shared zero origin; only the downcomer's
  −39 Pa actually diverged, and it compressed the axis to [−50,+20]. → replaced with a paired
  driver-vs-resistance chart (buoyancy magnitude vs mechanical total on separate baselines,
  sign kept in the label). No overlaps, clean 0→magnitude axis.

  labels on every bar, generous spacing, short caption, no heavy backgrounds.
