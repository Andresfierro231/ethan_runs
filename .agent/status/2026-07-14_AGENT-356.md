imposed heat by Salt case and physical leg. This is the best current
predictive-style model for the comparison, but it remains
`imposed_cooler_not_final_predictive_hx`.

Main finding: aggregate heat balance is close because leg-level errors cancel.

downcomer, but under-loses heat strongly in junction/stub/horizontal connector
regions.
