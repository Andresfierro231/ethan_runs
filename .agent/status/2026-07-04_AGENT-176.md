## Result

Main findings captured in the package:

- Best current evaluated Salt 2-4 compact form is `fit_major_k90_1p4`, with
  mean absolute mdot error `3.103%`.
- The best one-parameter major-loss fit, `fit_major_defaultK_1p4`, is nearly
  as good at `3.145%`; the two-parameter form improves by only `0.042`
  percentage points versus the best prior evaluated form.
- The Re-only / major-only proxy, `zero_minor_1p4_rad_on`, remains weak at
  `44.511%` mean absolute mdot error.
- The friction Re-only screen has `36` rows total and `12` rows usable under the
  current positive-friction/no-negative-flag gate. Only left lower/upper leg
  spans have enough usable Salt 2-4 nominal points for screen-only log-log
  fits; those fits are explicitly not publication-grade closure laws.
- The backlog records per-leg friction, per-leg f(Re), bend K(Re), thermal UA',
  upcomer recirculation, and Water validation as the next model-form/data lanes.
