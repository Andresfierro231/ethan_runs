## Taxonomy Extension

Added the resistance mental-model layer requested after the first pass:

- pressure/friction rows now carry `resistance_class`, `physics_role`,
  `development_state`, `buoyancy_role`, and `closure_admissibility`;
- thermal rows now carry `thermal_resistance_class`, `rom_energy_role`, and
  `nu_admissibility`;
- closure-observation rows carry both sets of taxonomy fields;
- `resistance_taxonomy_catalog.json` defines the reusable categories for
  buoyancy drive, distributed friction, minor/development losses, reversible
  acceleration, buoyancy-contaminated apparent resistance, recirculation-cell
  resistance, thermal resistance, and residuals.
