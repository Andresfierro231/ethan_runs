## Key Finding

`B3_imposed_setup_roles` matches imposed setup heat by construction, but differs
from realized CFD wall transfer by roughly 0.62 heater-power fractions at the
case-total level for Salt2-4. The largest segment residual is consistently the
upcomer/test-section lane, where imposed setup heat is positive but realized
CFD wall transfer is a sink. Passive downcomer and junction heat paths are also
missing from the imposed setup replay.
