`7` refreshed thermal rows. Publication-ready GCI rows remain `0`.
Fit-admissible thermal rows remain `0`.

Classification counts:

- `publication-ready GCI`: `0`
- `diagnostic-only`: `0`
- `blocked-sign-review`: `5`
- `blocked-downcomer-policy`: `1`
- `blocked-missing-triplet`: `5`
- `non-monotone/oscillatory`: `14`

The coarse thermal repair smoke closes the previous missing-coarse availability
gap for lower-leg and upcomer HTC/UA/Nu where values exist, but it does not
admit closure use. Thermal rows remain blocked by sign review, lower-leg Nu
availability/admission, and downcomer policy. Nonthermal closure rows remain
blocked by missing triplets or non-monotone/oscillatory behavior.

The admission table has `16` lower-leg/upcomer/downcomer rows covering
`wallHeatFlux`, enthalpy change, segment duty, HTC, UA_prime, and Nu:

- Fit-eligible rows: `0`
- Validation-only diagnostic rows: `11`
- Blocked rows: `5`
- Lower-leg: HTC/UA/wallHeatFlux/enthalpy/segment duty are validation-only;
  lower-leg Nu is blocked because the triplet is missing/nonfinite and direct
  Nu is not admitted.
- Upcomer: HTC/UA/Nu/wallHeatFlux/enthalpy/segment duty are validation-only
  recirculation/heat-balance diagnostics, not fit targets.
- Downcomer/right-leg: all rows are blocked by downcomer policy plus opposed
  wallHeatFlux/enthalpy direction, large residual, and high-recirculation
  interface flags.

Radiation semantics are preserved: CFD `rcExternalTemperature` includes
radiation in total `wallHeatFlux`, and no separate exported `qr` term exists.
Internal Nu/HTC/UA must not absorb heater, cooler, passive wall loss, wall
storage, junction, or radiation residuals.
