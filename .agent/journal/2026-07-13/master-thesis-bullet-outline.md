# Master's Thesis Bullet Outline

## Observed

The thesis dossier had a useful chapter skeleton, but it did not yet articulate
the work as a full thesis outline with explicit goals, model forms, inputs,
outputs, methodology, literature-review structure, research avenues, and
near-term achievable milestones.

## Action

Created `reports/thesis_dossier/master_thesis_bullet_outline.md` and linked it
from the main thesis dossier README.

## Interpretation

The current thesis should be framed around a branchwise, provenance-controlled
CFD-to-1D closure ledger and the transition from CFD-informed replay to a
predictive setup-only 1D model. The near-term two-week goal is not to claim a
fully validated predictive model at any cost; it is to produce a coherent model
contract, scorecard, literature-gated closure structure, and clear claim ledger
that distinguishes predictive, diagnostic, caveated, and blocked results.
